About Us

Our heritage, our passion

Our Story

Bin Rasheed Perfume House has been crafting exquisite Arabian fragrances for over three generations. Founded in the heart of the Middle East, our perfume house combines centuries-old traditions with modern perfumery techniques.

Each fragrance tells a story, capturing the essence of Arabian heritage and the spirit of luxury. Our master perfumers carefully select the finest ingredients from around the world to create scents that are both timeless and contemporary.
